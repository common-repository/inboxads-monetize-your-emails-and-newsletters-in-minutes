inboxAds - Monetize Your Emails and Newsletters in Minutes

== Description ==
Generate extra revenue for your business every time you send out a newsletter or other mass mailing with inboxAds. 

Sending mass emails and newsletters is an essential part of running a business. inboxAds monetizes those routine tasks, bringing in extra money each time you or your staff hit the send button.

**GENERATE REVENUE FROM EMAILS FOR FREE**
With no-cost registration and no limit on free ads, you can boost your revenue in only a few clicks. The only people who pay are the advertisers themselves.

**QUICK AND EASY REGISTRATION AND SETUP**
Simply sign up for your free inboxAds account. During the registration process, you’ll need to connect your account to the inboxAds plugin. You’ll be able to start monetizing your emails in minutes.

**USER-FRIENDLY**
With inboxAds’ user-friendly interface, just drag and drop the ad types you’d like to add to your newsletter. Ads that match your readers’ interest will appear in your email.

**WORKS WITH MOST WORDPRESS EMAIL PLUGINS – UNLIMITED PLUGINS**
Add as many newsletters and plugins you want. Our plugin is compatible with most of the popular WordPress plugins, including:
*   Newsletters
*   MailPoet
*   SendPress
*   ALO EasyMail
*   Mailster 
*   Newsletter

**SECURE TRANSACTIONS**
We’ve partnered with specialized payment providers to deliver enhanced security. inboxAds makes and receives all payments right on its website, saving your time. You can check your transaction history at any time, allowing you to keep track of your earnings.

**IAB STANDARDS SUPPORT**
We support all IAB ad size specifications. If none of those appeals to you, we also have a variety of customizable ad sizes and types.

**CURATED AD INVENTORY**
We select only premium advertisers to fill ad slots on your emails. Our AI-powered ad selection chooses only ads that will appeal to your recipients—and we curate each of your ads before your readers see them.

**HIGH PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS**
Track your ads’ revenue and impressions during the past seven days with our easy-to-read performance chart. For a deeper dive into the data, use your publisher dashboard to analyze the numbers. Learn which emails and newsletters perform best so you can find your top performers and tweak your underperformers.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP version 7.1 or greater
* MySQL version 5.7 or greater

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don’t need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of "inboxAds", log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "inboxAds" and click Search Plugins. Once you’ve found the plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking “Install Now”.

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our plugin and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application OR from Admin Area -> Plugins -> Add New and choose 'Upload Plugin'. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).
